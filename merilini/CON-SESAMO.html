Organoleptic
characteristics		 Thin mini-breadsticks with surface dusted with durum wheat semolina flour from
Altamura (Italy). Its straw-yellow colour is due to the long fermentation
processes to which the mixes are subjected; this gives the product the distinctive
flavour with hints of yeast. The addition of the sesame seeds gives to the
breadsticks their typical and pleasant flavor. The hand-craftsmanship, with the
necessary time for the maturation of the mixes, raises the quality standards and
allows to give to the product its uniqueness. The result is a natural digestibility of
an appetizing delicacy, without further addition.
